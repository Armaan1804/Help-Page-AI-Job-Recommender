AI Job Recommender
How It Works

Our AI Job Recommender is here to help you recommend you jobs based on your resume.

1. Upload Your Resume

  • • The first step is to upload you resume on the "Browse files" Button.
  • • Select your resume.
  • • In the end, click on "upload" to upload you resume

2. Get your Resume Summary

  • Scroll Down and our AI Job Recommender will give you the summary of your Resume

3. Find your Skill Gaps and Missing Areas

  • Scroll Down Further and our AI Job Recommender will give you the summary of your Skill gaps and Missing Areas in your Resume.

4. Click on Get Job Recommendations

  • • The next step is to click "Get Job Recommendations" Button.
  • • It will show you your extracted keywords from you resume
  • • In the end, upload your resume

5. View your Job Recommendations

  • • After Clicking on Get Job Recommendations.
  • • Scroll Down Further and our AI Job Recommender will give provide you with your Job Recommendations.
  • • Select your resume.
